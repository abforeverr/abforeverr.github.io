Sagan Ceremony & Sangeet

Date: 9th December 2024

Sagan Ceremony

A pre-wedding ritual where the bride and the groom are blessed by both the families, as they receive gifts and sweets symbolizing good luck. Join us for this auspicious ceremony as we shower blessings upon the lovely couple.

A musical celebration before the wedding where both families sing, dance, and enjoy performances to celebrate the upcoming marriage.
